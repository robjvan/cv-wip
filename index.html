Skills

Systems Administration

Familiar with Windows Server 2008 R2/2012 and all workstation versions from XP to 10. Past experience managing AD DS, DHCP, DNS, WSUS, File and Print server roles. Familiar with SCCM, Cisco UCM, and BES. Highly experienced in IMAC, OS migration, imaging, and managing inventory. Working knowledge of Linux and MacOS systems.

Development

I've dabbled in both front and back end development, having played with Java and C# for applications and HTML/CSS for websites. Successful projects include an in-house tool using C# that captured and restored an enterprise user's data to streamline the transition to a new OS or workstation.

Hardware Repair

Having over a decade of experience tinkering and repairing electronics means I can fix just about anything. I've built, maintained, or repaired hundreds of units including workstations, laptops, servers, phones, tablets, televisions, stereos, peripheral devices, and even small home appliances.

Technical Support

Over 5 years of experience as Tier 1 and 2 technical support. I have helped clients over the phone, through email, via video chat, and in person. I specialize in field support having traveled throughout western Canada working with clients in the energy, financial, government, retail, and food service industries. I maintain a reputation for excepetional customer experience through empathy, accountability, transparency, and efficient service.

Technical Writing

Over the years, I've created process documentation, training leaflets, manuals, KB articles, and mass business communications for many different clients. I value clear and concise instructions that provide a bit of insight to avoid future problems and simplify ease of use.

3D Visualizations

Trained in 3ds max and Photoshop, I have also used Rhino, Bryce, AutoCAD, and SolidWorks. This blend of software lends itself well to photo-realistic visualizations and protoyping.

"Jack Of All Trades"

I've had various seasonal or temporary jobs to fill gaps. Some examples include shipper/receiver, order picker, painting masker, metal building erector, roof flashings installer, irrigation installer, fish wrangler, CNC machine operator, assembler, and delivery driver.

Vehicle Maintenance/Repair

While I haven't had the chance to do too much, I enjoy tinkering with my own car and performing the required maintenance and repairs including fluids changes, brake jobs, and part swaps.
